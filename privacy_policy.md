# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: November 07, 2025**

Gilman Technologies, Inc. ("**we**," "**us**," or "**our**") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use GRIX (the "**App**"), available exclusively on the Apple App Store in the United States.

By downloading, installing, or using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or share any personal information.**

The App enables **direct peer-to-peer (P2P) audio and video communication** between two devices running the App. All audio, video, and signaling data is exchanged **exclusively between the connected peers** and is **never transmitted to, stored on, or processed by any server, third party, or us**.

| Data Type                                | Collected?                        | Purpose                  | Linked to User? | Used for Tracking? |
|------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------|--------------------------|-----------------|---------------------|
| Personal identifiers (name, email, etc.) | No                                | —                        | —               | —                   |
| Device identifiers (IDFA, etc.)          | No                                | —                        | —               | —                   |
| Audio / video streams                    | No (P2P only)                     | —                        | —               | —                   |
| IP addresses / network info              | Transient during P2P session only | Facilitate connection    | No              | No                  |
| Analytics / crash data                   | No                                | —                        | —               | —                   |

---

## 2. Peer-to-Peer Communication

- The App uses Custom WebSocket + peer-to-peer streaming + Bonjour to discover and connect nearby devices.
- **IP addresses and temporary session identifiers** are exchanged **only between the two peers** to establish the connection.
- As soon as the session ends, this transient data is **discarded** and is **never logged or retained**.

---

## 3. No Third-Party Services

The App does **not** integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

---

## 4. Data Security

Although no data is stored, we implement industry-standard security measures in the App to protect the integrity of P2P sessions, including:

- Encrypted transport (WSS, DTLS/SRTP where applicable)
- Secure peer discovery

## 5. Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. It is a simple P2P communication tool with no account creation or data storage.
